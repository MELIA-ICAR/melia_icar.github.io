# MELIA Unit – ICAR NIAP

## Monitoring, Evaluation, Learning and Impact Assessment (MELIA) Unit

The official web portal of the **Monitoring, Evaluation, Learning and Impact Assessment (MELIA) Unit** established under the **ICAR – National Institute of Agricultural Economics and Policy Research (NIAP), New Delhi, India**.

The MELIA Unit has been created to strengthen evidence-based planning, institutional learning, monitoring systems, programme evaluation, and impact assessment across the agricultural research and education ecosystem of ICAR.

---

## About the Unit

The MELIA Unit supports the development of robust monitoring and evaluation frameworks for agricultural research, development programmes, and policy interventions. The Unit promotes data-driven governance, institutional accountability, and evidence-based decision-making through advanced analytics, reporting systems, and impact assessment methodologies.

The Unit also facilitates:
- Institutional learning and knowledge management
- Data integration and reporting systems
- Monitoring and evaluation of agricultural programmes
- Impact assessment of ICAR technologies and interventions
- Development of dashboards and visual analytics
- Policy research support and evidence generation
